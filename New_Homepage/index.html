Everything you need to know about refurbished devices

Refurbished devices are pre-owned electronics that have been professionally inspected, tested, cleaned, and restored to like-new condition. Every device goes through our rigorous 45-point inspection process and comes with a 1-year warranty.

Every purchase includes the device, a charging cable, 1-year warranty, 30-day money-back guarantee, and free shipping on orders over ₹40,000. Some devices may also include original accessories.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you're not completely satisfied, simply return the device in its original condition for a full refund.

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available at checkout. All orders over ₹40,000 qualify for free standard shipping.

Most of our phones are carrier-unlocked and will work with any carrier. Product listings clearly indicate if a device is locked to a specific carrier.
